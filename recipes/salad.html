Salad Recipe

You want a healty diet that comes with a fishy situation? you come to the right place!

Ingredients
  • 1 egg
  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
    • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
Steps
  1. Place egg in a saucepan and cover with cold water.
  2. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let egg stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes
  3. Remove from hot water; cool for 5 minutes. Peel and chop into bite-sized pieces.
  4. In a medium bowl, mix together tuna and mayonnaise. Mix in egg, celery, relish, and black pepper.
